Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Wear and tear caused by regular use of the helmet can take a toll on the linings and paddings. Over a certain period of time the interior part of the helmet can start to peel off or disintegrate, which will not only make it uncomfortable for you, but it also can be potentially hazardous because small parts peeling off from the helmet can enter your ears or nose and cause a distraction when you are riding the motorcycle. If your helmet comes with replaceable inner linings and paddings, consider replacing it immediately otherwise, get a new helmet.
